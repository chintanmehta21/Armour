About

Armour was developed by four tech-savvy enthusiasts as a part of their final year project. But as we grew, amazing people like you hopped in the ride and our family grew bigger. We are basically a security-providing company. I know that is likely an over-simplified explanation but is still better than a long list of descriptors.
